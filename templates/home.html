This website is used as an example on how to use Burp's Custom Iterator.

Scenario

Imagine you are testing a website for a department store, and you want to evaluate if there are any IDOR issues. The problem is that the format for the invoices are not a simple number. The example you have looks like 20211105|22|03.

Through your investigations you have figured out:

  • First part is the date: YYYYMMDD
  • You don't know the second part, but you have noticed that it has never been larger than 29
  • Third part is the zero-padded store number: 03
  • The total number of stores is 8 and the first store number is 01

Link to Example Invoice

Challenge

Based on this information, you want to find all invoices for sales done this year. Once you hit the right combination a response will be shown.
